Deep Sleep Analysis - Gold AI
Example of a GOLD data insight:  Using the first consumer EEG sleep tracker ‘Zeo’ made possible by Dave Dickinson for an early discovery of room temperature and deep sleep. This insight was used to cool the sleeping temperatures of athletes using a water-cooled mattress topper to increase deep sleep to enhance recovery, which increased training output, eventually helping win medals at the London Olympics.
